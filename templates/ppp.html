What is Performance Progression Percentile (PPP)?
TLDR: In essence, PPP is the percent of players that you would rank higher than if every player were to have the same playing time as you.

Why is my percentile so low/high when I've progressed fast?
The regression is mostly for five-digits or lower. Due to the high inactivity and high variance within six digits and higher, six-digit percentiles may be more inaccurate.
